WHAT WE DO

Our Story

We started because filament was too expensive and plastic waste was too ignored.

Respool was founded by a group of engineers and makers who were tired of paying high prices for imported filament, and watching recyclable plastic end up in landfills. We decided to fix both at once.

Our Mission

To make 3D printing accessible to everyone — students, makers, professionals, and small businesses — by closing the loop on plastic waste and turning it into the raw material of creation. Every spool sold is plastic that did not go to a landfill.

LOCALIZED ACQUISITION

Intercepting waste vectors inside municipal boundaries, eliminating external shipping pollution.

FILTRATION SPECS

Strict polymer purity scans ensure no hazardous gases are produced during final melting phases.

UNIVERSAL ACCESS

Diverting material yields to high-density spool runs distributed locally at minimal cost margins.
